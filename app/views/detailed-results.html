Your water availability report

This report shows surface water and groundwater availability for your abstraction site and explains next steps in applying for a water abstraction licence.

National grid reference: TQ 185 769

Proposed abstraction volume: 100 cubic metres a day

Duration: Proposed volume available for 8 months of the year

Surface water and groundwater availability

Water can be abstracted from surface water, such as a river, and from groundwater, such as an aquifer.

Surface water availability for your site

Surface water availability depends upon the flow rate of the surface water source you abstract from.

Low flows are usually in summer and high flows in winter.

Summary of flow rate and water availability for grid reference TQ 185 769
Surface water flow rate Water available for abstraction
Low flow No water available
Medium flow Water available at less than 100 cubic metres a day
High flow Water available at 100 cubic metres a day

Groundwater availability for your site

Groundwater for grid reference TQ 185 769 is: Available to abstract

Next steps

The Environment Agency grants water abstraction licences. When considering whether to grant a licence for your site, we consider other factors as well as groundwater and surface water availability.

Those other factors include the impact of the proposed abstraction on:

  • nearby abstractors
  • the natural environment
