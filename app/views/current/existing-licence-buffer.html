You may be able to vary your existing licence

If you are taking water from the same source (so a point from the same water body, in close proximity to your existing licensed abstraction point) you can usually apply for a variation to your existing licence. This is often cheaper and simpler than a new licence application.

You only need to apply for a new licence if you are taking water from a new source or a different water body. This usually applies to bigger organisations working across multiple sites or on large plots of land.
