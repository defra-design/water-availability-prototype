Check if you are exempt from needing a licence

You probably will not need a licence if you want to abstract (take) less than 20 cubic metres of water a day.

Certain other uses are also exempt from needing a licence for abstraction, including water used for fire fighting, drinking water on boats, and water used for low risk passive dewatering.

Most standard commercial or farming uses like spray irrigation and mining will require a licence.

Check if your intended water use is listed as an exemption.
