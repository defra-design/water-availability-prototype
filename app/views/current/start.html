Water abstraction is the removal of water from natural sources such as rivers, lakes and groundwater to use in agriculture, industry and public supply.

Use this service to:

  • check water availability in your local area
  • help to decide whether to apply for a water abstraction licence
  • find out the range of water supply options available to you

You cannot use this service to:

  • check water availability for an exact location
  • find the best point in your area to take water
  • get a definite answer about whether you will be granted a licence
  • apply for a licence

To check water availability in your local area, you will need one of the following:

  • postcode
  • national grid reference
  • field parcel number
  • existing licence number (if you plan to take more water from an existing abstraction point)
