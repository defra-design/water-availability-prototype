Make sure you do not need a licence before abstracting water

It's important you comply with water and environmental regulations. Make sure you are definitely exempt from licensing and consider whether you need any other environmental permits or planning permissions before you abstract water.

Check if your intended water use is listed as an exemption.

The Environment Agency carry out site inspections to check compliance. If you commit an offence they may take enforcement action. More information on fines and convictions are listed in the water resources offence response options.

If you are unsure and need further guidance on whether you need a licence, contact the Environment Agency.
